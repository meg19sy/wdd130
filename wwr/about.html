White Water Rafting

Unleash your inner adventurer with River Rush! Since 2010, we've been your one-stop shop for unforgettable white-water rafting experiences. We offer thrilling trips for all skill levels, from family-friendly floats to heart-pounding rapids. Join us and discover why River Rush has been paddling strong for over a decade!

History

  • 2010: Riding the rapids of growth. We expand our listings to include outfitters across North America.
  • 2012: Navigating new waters. We introduce trip reviews and expert rafting guides to help you plan your adventure.
  • 2014: Paddling forward with safety. We prioritize safety information and certification listings for outfitters.
  • 2016: Making waves in the industry. We become a leading resource for white-water rafting enthusiasts.
  • 2019: Charting a course for all. We expand our reach to international rafting destinations.
  • 2023: Still going strong! We remain your one-stop shop for unforgettable white-water rafting experiences.
